# Rank and Rent Compliance Guidelines

## Core Rules

### Never Claim Past Projects or Specific Clients
- Do not reference specific past projects or client names
- Do not use testimonials or case studies with identifiable information
- Frame scenarios as examples of the type of engagement we can handle

### Example Capability Framework
- All examples must include: "Example of the type of engagement we can handle"
- Examples should be generic and educational
- Invite contact to share real references upon request
- Do not fabricate timelines or outcomes

### No Unverifiable Claims
- No testimonials
- No review stars or ratings
- No badges without verifiable sources
- No claims about specific results or outcomes

### Tax and Legal Disclaimers
- Educational content only, not tax, legal, or investment advice
- 1031 defers income tax on qualifying real property
- 1031 does not remove transfer or documentary taxes
- Always include compliance notes where appropriate

### Securities Disclaimers
- If DST or TIC is mentioned, include: "DST or TIC may be securities. We do not sell securities. We provide introductions to licensed providers only."
- Do not market securities
- Provide introductions to licensed providers only

### Footer Disclosure
All pages must include:
```
Disclosure: This site routes inquiries to our chosen fulfillment partner for 1031 exchange advisory and property identification support.
```

### Content Standards
- Plain English, no contractions
- No em dashes
- Technical accuracy required
- Do not make up information, especially for locations
- Include city and state in required places
- Follow Hobo Technical SEO 2025 best practices
